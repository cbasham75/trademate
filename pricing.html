FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a credit card to start? ↓
No. Your 30-day free trial starts the moment you create an account. No credit card required until you decide to subscribe.
What happens when my trial ends? ↓
Your account switches to read-only mode — you can still view all your quotes, invoices, and customers but won't be able to create new ones until you subscribe. Nothing gets deleted.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Annual plans are billed once per year — if you cancel an annual plan your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What trades is Grarose built for? ↓
Grarose works for any contractor or trade professional — electrical, plumbing, HVAC, structured cabling, AV, security, general contracting, painting, landscaping, and more. If you quote jobs and send invoices, Grarose is for you.
How does SMS sending work? ↓
Grarose sends SMS messages from a dedicated toll-free number on your behalf. Your customers receive quotes, invoices, and overdue reminders via text. They can reply STOP at any time to opt out.
Is my data secure? ↓
Yes. All data is stored securely in Supabase with encrypted connections. We never sell your data or your customers' data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
